About Us

Welcome to LensCraft Studio, where artistry meets photography to capture life’s most cherished moments. Nestled in the heart of Cuba Street, Wellington, our boutique studio is dedicated to providing a unique and creative approach to every shoot. Whether it’s a wedding, a family portrait, or a commercial campaign, our passion is to deliver timeless, high-quality imagery that tells your story.

At LensCraft Studio, we believe that photography is more than just taking pictures. It’s about creating lasting memories through a lens of creativity. We specialize in intimate and artistic portraits, capturing the essence of who you are and celebrating your most important milestones. Our team is committed to making your experience not only enjoyable but truly memorable, from the first consultation to the final product.

We understand the importance of each moment we capture, which is why we take the time to get to know you and your vision. Whether you're an engaged couple looking for breathtaking wedding photos, a family wanting to preserve special memories, or a business seeking high-quality commercial imagery, we’ve got you covered.

Thank you for considering LensCraft Studio. We can’t wait to be part of your next special moment.
